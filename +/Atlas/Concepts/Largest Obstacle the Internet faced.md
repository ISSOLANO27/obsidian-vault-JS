> The largest obstacle to effectively use the Internet is it sheer Scope and Size.

The solution to the problem was developed in 1989 by Timothy Berners-Lee and other researchers at the CERN nuclear facility near Geneva, Switzerland.

What they needed was an Information System that would allow their researchers to locate and share data! on the CERN Network. 

hence, **Hypertext** was created.

It was ideally suited for the Internet since end users do not need to know where a service is located. All they needed to know was how to activate the link.

Adoption happened fast across the internet. The totality was known as the World Wide Web
