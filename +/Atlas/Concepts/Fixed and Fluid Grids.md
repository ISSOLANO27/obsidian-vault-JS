Grids are often used with responsive design in which 1 grid layout is used with **mobile devices**, another grid layout is used with **tablets**, and yet another layout is used with **desktop computers**. 

A layout for a mobile device is typically based on a **1-column grid**, 

tablet layouts are based on grids of **4 to 12 columns**, 

...and desktop layouts are often based on layouts with **12 or more** columns.
