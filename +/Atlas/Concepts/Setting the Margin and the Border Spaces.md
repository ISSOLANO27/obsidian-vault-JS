similar to setting up the padding. To se the size of the margin around your block elements:

`margin: size;`

or

`margin: top right bottom left`

individual sides:

```css
margin-top: size;
margin-right: size;
margin-bottom: size;
margin-left: size;
```
Size is expressed in one of CSS units of length or the `auto`, to automatically set the margin.

---
[[border-width]]

`border-width: size;

`border-width: top right bottom left`

**properties**:
```css
border-top-width
border-right-width 
border-bottom-width 
border-left-width 
```
