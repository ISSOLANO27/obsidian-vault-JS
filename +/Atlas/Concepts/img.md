#### Image Size

Size matches that of the image, but you can specify another size:

```html
width="value" height="value"` 
```
#emptyElements 

They are expressed in pixels.

Just like the default size, 

if you adjust the height, the Browser will maintain the width in proportion of the image. 

---
img tags are also known as **inline images** since they are placed, like **text level elements**, in line with surrounding content. #textLevelElements
