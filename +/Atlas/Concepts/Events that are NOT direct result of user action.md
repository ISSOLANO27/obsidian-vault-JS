[[load]] is another monitored event by JS.

Note that not all events happen with all devices. For instance, **keydown** and **keyup** are triggered **only by a keyboard**.

**touchend**, **touchmove**, and **touchstart** take place **ONLY** on a touchscreen device. 

For this reason, it’s important to choose trigger events that make your scripts available to **users on all devices**. You’ll explore different methods of doing this as you build your JavaScript skills.
