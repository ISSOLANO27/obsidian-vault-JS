Cookies are small pieces of text stored by the browser.

example:
`sessionId = 92ks8dj29
`
They are sent automatically with requests to the same domain.

Purpose:
- Identify sessions
- keep users logged in
- store preferences

JS can read/write cookies unless they are marked as HttpOnly (which blocks JS access for security)

other storage:
- localStorage
- sessionStorage
- IndexDB
These are controlled browser storage systems.

Not your hard drive.

Not your system files.
