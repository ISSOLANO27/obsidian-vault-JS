HTML provides several attributes to the [[a]] element that control the behavior and appearance of hypertext links. 

### Attributes of the a Element

|Attribute|Description|
|---|---|
|href=“_url_”|Provides the _url_ of the hypertext link|
|target=(_blank\|_parent\|_self\|_top)|Specifies where to open the linked document|
|download=“_filename_”|Indicates that the link should be downloaded as a file, where _filename_ is the name given to the downloaded file|
|rel=“_type_”|Provides the relationship between the linked document and the current page|
|hreflang=“_lang_”|Indicates the language of the linked document|
|type=“_mime-type_”|Indicates the media type of the linked document|
