In the following statement, an alert box is displayed with the message “Enjoy your free shipping” if primeMember is true or the message “Go prime for free shipping” if it is false:
```js
(primeMember) ? window.alert("Enjoy your free shipping") : 
				window.alert("Go prime for free shipping");
```

> Note: that long expressions are often more easily written on several lines. As long as the text strings are all contained on a single line, you can break the statement at other points for better readability.
