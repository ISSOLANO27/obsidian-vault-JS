That is fundamentally different from designing for print, where the page size is fixed and guaranteed.

Now here’s the deeper conceptual shift:

Web design is **constraint-aware engineering**.

You must plan for:
- Extremely narrow viewports
- Extremely wide viewports
- Users resizing windows
- Accessibility zoom
- Device rotation (portrait vs landscape)
    
This is why fluid layouts, elastic layouts, and modern responsive design exist. They are strategies for surviving variability.
