**Absolute units** are units that are **fixed in size regardless of the output device** and are usually used only with printed media. 

They are specified in one of five standard units of measurement: 
- `mm (millimeters)`
- `cm (centimeters)`
- `in (inches)`
- `pt (points)`
- `pc (picas)`
