Web page layouts fall into three general categories: [[fixed]], [[fluid]], and [[elastic]]. 


With **different devices accessing your website**, it’s usually best to work with a [[fluid]] layout that is more adaptable to a range of screen resolutions. 

Fixed layouts should only be used when **you have more control over the devices** that will display your page, such as a web page created specifically for a digital kiosk at a conference.

> The web is moving quickly toward the principles of [[responsive design]].
